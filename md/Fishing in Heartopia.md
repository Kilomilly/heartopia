Fish can be caught all throughout Heartopia, and is unlocked by the beginning of the game's tutorial (After helping Vanya repair his fishing rod) / Developer Level 2.

How to Fish
You can see the buoy when you are holding a fishing rod near the water surface. Try to get the buoy close to the fish shadow.
Hold the Cast button in the bottom right corner for more precise casting position adjustment.
Pay attention to the Fishing Line color during fishing. Release appropriately when the line turns red.
Using Bait: Approach the water, cast the bait, and fish will be drawn to the ripples, Mermaid Fish Attractors are used similarly.
To watch the tutorial clips in game go to the guide summary section (top right button under Town Guide app on your phone) or select the book icon within the Fishing section of your Hobby app.
