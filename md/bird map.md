Heartopia Winter Birds Location Map: Complete Guide & All Spawns
Winter Birds are exclusive to Heartopia's Winter Frost Season, appearing as special variants (often with hats/accessories) of regular birds like mallards, flamingos, and others. They're key for Birdwatching Hobby tasks, earning Festival Tokens, unlocking bird cages/furniture, and exhibition progress. This page is your one-stop map and guide—updated February 9, 2026 with all confirmed locations from GameRant, YouTube, Reddit, and FB groups.

How to Catch/Scan Winter Birds (Step-by-Step)

Unlock Birdwatching Lv2 (complete basic scans at Research Lab).
Upgrade Net Lv2+ (shop or lab).
Check Weather (Watch app: Sunny/Rainy best, Snowing for variants).
Go to Hotspots (map pins: lakes/forests—catch common birds first to trigger rares).
Approach & Catch: Crouch/sneak → swing net or camera scan/photo when close.
Submit: Bailey NPC or Collections app for rewards.

Time: 10-20 mins for all. Respawn: Catch commons to encourage.

Rewards & Quests for Winter Birds

Per Bird: 100-300 Festival Tokens + Bird Info Card progress.
Full Collection: Achievement + bird cage furniture + exhibition bonus.
Quests: "On Duty" tasks (scan 5 winter birds) → Tokens + decor.

Tips, Tricks & Troubleshooting

No Spawns?: Catch common mallards/flamingos first; relog or change weather/area.
Hard to Catch?: Crouch sneak; Lv2 net essential.
Missed Season?: Friends' houses with bird ornaments for scans.
Best Route: Start Forest Lake (Smew) → Deer Tower (Wonga) → Panda Forest (Wigeon).
