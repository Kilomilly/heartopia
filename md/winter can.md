H2: What is Watering Crops in Heartopia?
Watering crops is a core Gardening Lv1 task: Use watering can on dry garden plots to make them sparkle (wet). Required for quests, crop growth, and Gourmet Life progression. Issues: Plots don't count, rain/snow blocks, multi-water fails.
Quick Facts:

Tool: Watering Can (Toolbox equip)
Plots: Dry = brown; Wet = sparkling
Lv3 Unlock: Hold to water multiple plots

H2: Step-by-Step: How to Water Crops with Watering Can

Equip Watering Can: Toolbox (right side) → Select watering can icon → Equip.
Approach Plot: Walk near dry garden plot (sparkle indicator shows range).
Water: Tap watering can icon (mobile) or right-click (PC) → Plot sparkles = success.
Lv3 Multi-Water: Hold icon (Lv3 unlocked) → Waters 3-5 adjacent plots at once.
Quest Check: Quest log updates (sparkling = counted).
